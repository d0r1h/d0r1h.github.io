## Abstract

This research presents the Indian Legal Corpus (ILC), a specialized dataset created for the purpose of summarizing Indian legal proceedings using natural language processing techniques. The dataset addresses the unique challenges of processing legal documents in the Indian context, including multilingual content and complex legal terminology.

## Key Contributions

- Created a comprehensive corpus of Indian legal documents
- Developed summarization techniques tailored for legal text
- Addressed challenges specific to Indian legal proceedings
- Provided benchmarks for legal document summarization

## Research Context

This work was conducted at SCAAI (Symbiosis Centre for Applied Artificial Intelligence) under the guidance of Dr. Shila Gite, focusing on applying NLP techniques to the legal domain.
